# How to Open Website in Cursor Browser

## ✅ Both Servers Are Running!

- **Backend:** http://localhost:3001
- **Frontend:** http://localhost:3000

---

## Method 1: Using Cursor's Simple Browser (Easiest)

1. **Press `Ctrl+Shift+P`** (or `Cmd+Shift+P` on Mac)
2. **Type:** `Simple Browser: Show`
3. **Enter URL:** `http://localhost:3000`
4. Press Enter

The website will open in Cursor's built-in browser!

---

## Method 2: Using Launch Configuration

1. **Press `F5`** or go to **Run and Debug** (Ctrl+Shift+D)
2. **Select:** "Launch SkillSwap Frontend" from dropdown
3. **Click the play button** ▶️

This will open Chrome with your website.

---

## Method 3: Right-Click on URL

1. In any terminal output, **right-click** on `http://localhost:3000`
2. Select **"Open in Browser"** or **"Open Link"**

---

## Method 4: Command Palette

1. **Press `Ctrl+Shift+P`**
2. **Type:** `Open Preview` or `Browser Preview`
3. Enter: `http://localhost:3000`

---

## Quick Access

Once servers are running, you can:

- **Frontend:** http://localhost:3000
- **Backend API:** http://localhost:3001/health

Just paste these URLs in Cursor's Simple Browser!

---

## Tip

You can also use the **Simple Browser** icon in Cursor's activity bar (left sidebar) if available.
